Tash Logan Dr Tash Logan

Dr. Logan graduated from Murdoch University in Perth, Western Australia with a Bachelor of Science (Bsc)in Chiropractic Science and Bachelor of Clinical Chiropractic (BClinChiro) .

Dr. Logan’s passion is ensuring the human body is functioning at its absolute optimum capacity, allowing you to lead your life to the fullest. Being an elite athlete in her child and teen years, participating in various forms of martial arts, she developed a strong awareness of the body and the importance of it being at its prime every day. Chiropractic allowed Dr Logan to achieve this level of overall wellness, inspiring her to pursue a career in Chiropractic.

Being a passionate sports enthusiast Dr. Logan has extensive experience as a Sports Trainer working at various soccer clubs, including the A-league team Perth Glory, and Chiropractor at the Victorian NPL club Heidelberg United. With this experience, she has a broad knowledge in the management and prevention of musculoskeletal injuries with vast experience in rehabilitation, soft tissue therapy and taping. Dr Logan uses hands on chiropractic techniques for her patients including manual chiropractic adjustments (diversified), drop piece, spinal and limb mobilisations, activator, pelvic blocking, percussion soft tissue therapy, and rehabilitative stretches and exercises to treat various clinical cases. Dr Logan has a strong belief in treating an issue at its the primary source. It is important to treat the region of pain, however the cause must be targeted for longevity of optimum health!
